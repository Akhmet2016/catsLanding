INFO

Siberian cats are Russia’s native forest cats and are known to have existed for a long time in the dense forests of Siberia. The earliest known reference is from 1000 AD. Outside of Russia, the Siberian cat was first mentioned in an 1892 book by Harrison Weir, who organized and wrote about some of the earliest cat shows in England in 1871.
